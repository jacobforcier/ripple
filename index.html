Questions

Good questions,
honest answers.

Do my friends know it's an affiliate link?

Yes. Every Ripple link redirects through a brief disclosure page that clearly states you may earn a small commission if they purchase. Transparency is built in — it's not optional.

Do I need to sign up for Amazon Associates or any affiliate programs?

No. Ripple handles all affiliate program relationships on the backend. You sign up for Ripple once, and every supported retailer is automatically covered.

How much can I actually earn?

Commissions vary by retailer — typically 1–8% of the purchase price. On most products, you're looking at cents to a few dollars per sale. The value is in volume: a single strong recommendation to a group can add up quickly.

When and how do I get paid?

You accumulate earnings in your Ripple dashboard and can cash out once you hit a minimum threshold. We're finalizing the payout flow — sign up to be notified when it launches.
